Orders & Delivery

How can I track my bespoke shipment?

Once your selection has been curated and prepared, a private tracking link will be dispatched to your registered email. For high-value atelier pieces, a personal concierge will oversee the final-mile delivery.

International duties and taxes?

All prices displayed for international destinations are inclusive of landed costs. VIBEMALL handles all customs clearance to ensure a seamless arrival at your doorstep.

The Collection

Authenticity and craftsmanship?

Every item in VIBEMALL is sourced directly from certified artisans or official brand partners. Each piece arrives with a physical Certificate of Provenance secured by a unique digital signature.

Can I request a private viewing?

Private digital viewings for high-jewelry and couture pieces can be scheduled via our Personal Shopper service available in the Profile section.
